Common Roofing Problems

Leaking Roof

A leaking roof is one of the most common roofing problems homeowners face. Water can seep through damaged shingles, cracked flashing, or deteriorated underlayment, causing damage to ceilings, walls, and insulation. Ignoring a leak can lead to mold growth, structural damage, and costly repairs. Have questions about your roof and leaks? Give us a call and we’ll be happy to answer.

Missing or Damaged Shingles

Shingles are your roof’s first line of defense against the elements. Over time, shingles can become damaged by wind, hail, sun exposure, and normal wear and tear. Missing or damaged shingles expose the underlayment to the elements, increasing the risk of leaks and structural damage. Replacing missing or damaged shingles promptly can prevent further problems and extend the life of your roof. It is possible to do it yourself but may be dangerous, allow our trained staff to get the job done. Give us a call today and allow us to help you.

Damaged Flashing

Flashing is a thin material, usually metal, that is installed around chimneys, vents, skylights, and other roof penetrations to prevent water from entering. Damaged or deteriorated flashing can allow water to seep into your home, causing leaks and water damage. Regularly inspecting and maintaining your flashing can help prevent costly repairs and extend the life of your roof. Allow Biloxi Roofing to assist you today! Click the call button below.

Call Now: (228) 296-1499

Clogged Gutters

Clogged gutters can cause water to back up onto your roof, leading to leaks and water damage. When gutters are filled with leaves, debris, and other obstructions, they cannot effectively channel water away from your foundation. This can result in water damage to your siding, foundation, and landscaping. Cleaning your gutters regularly or installing gutter guards can prevent clogs and protect your home from water damage. We not only clean the gutters but can help determine what materials are best, give us a call today!

Sagging Roof

A sagging roof is a serious structural issue that requires immediate attention. Sagging can be caused by water damage, rot, or inadequate support. Ignoring a sagging roof can lead to further structural damage and even collapse. If you notice any signs of sagging, it is important to contact a qualified roofing contractor immediately to assess the problem and recommend appropriate repairs. Biloxi Roofing is here to ensure that your roof doesn’t sag! Give us a call today to prevent costly future repairs!

Signs You Need Roofing Services

Water Stains on Ceilings or Walls

Water stains are a telltale sign of a leaking roof. If you notice water stains on your ceilings or walls, it is important to investigate the source of the leak and take prompt action to prevent further damage. Water stains can indicate a variety of roofing problems, from minor leaks to severe structural damage. Don’t let mother nature tell you that your roof sucks, give us a call and we can assist you.

Visible Mold Growth

Mold thrives in damp, dark environments. If you notice visible mold growth on your ceilings, walls, or in your attic, it is a sign that you have a moisture problem. Mold can cause health problems and structural damage, so it is important to address the problem quickly. A leaking roof is a common cause of mold growth in homes. Granules in Gutters

Asphalt shingles are coated with granules that protect them from the sun’s harmful UV rays. Over time, these granules can become loose and wash into your gutters. Excessive granule loss can shorten the lifespan of your shingles and increase the risk of leaks. So call us to replace those damages and get your roof the help it needs!

Curling or Buckling Shingles

Curling or buckling shingles are a sign that your roof is nearing the end of its lifespan. As shingles age, they can become brittle and prone to curling or buckling. This can expose the underlayment to the elements and increase the risk of leaks. Replacing curling or buckling shingles can prevent further damage and extend the life of your roof. It’s not worth the risk to do yourself, call an expert to get the job done! Biloxi Roofing is here to help make your worries go away!

Increased Energy Bills

A damaged or poorly insulated roof can cause your energy bills to increase. If you notice a sudden spike in your energy consumption, it is important to inspect your roof for signs of damage. Leaks, missing shingles, and inadequate insulation can all contribute to energy loss. Fixing these problems can help you save money on your energy bills and improve the comfort of your home. Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my roof inspected?

It’s recommended to have your roof inspected at least once a year, preferably in the spring and fall. Regular inspections can help identify potential problems early on and prevent costly repairs. Additionally, it’s a good idea to schedule an inspection after any severe weather event, such as a hurricane or hail storm. The earlier you catch issues, the easier they are on the wallet!

How long does a roof replacement take?

The duration of a roof replacement depends on various factors, including the size and complexity of your roof, the type of roofing materials used, and the weather conditions. In general, most roof replacements can be completed in 1-3 days. We will provide you with a detailed timeline before starting the project so you know what to expect. You want more details on the roofing process? Give us a call!

What type of roofing material is best for my home?

The best roofing material for your home depends on your budget, aesthetic preferences, and the local climate. Asphalt shingles are a popular and affordable option, while metal roofs are more durable and energy-efficient. Tile roofs are a beautiful and long-lasting choice, but they are also more expensive. Our roofing experts can help you weigh the pros and cons of each material and choose the best option for your needs. Let Biloxi Roofing guide you and call us now!
